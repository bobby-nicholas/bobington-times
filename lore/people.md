# People

## Public Figures
- **Mayor Harriet Blackthorne** — Current mayor of Bobington. Supportive of tramway expansion. Presenting Freedom of the City to the Rovers on Tuesday 18 Feb.
- **Council Speaker Desmond Falk** — Presides over City Council
- **Councilwoman Ida Pryce** — Led the tramway campaign for 8 years. District includes the Docklands. Called for building inspection review after Greystone fire.
- **Councilman Aldric Voss** — Vocal opponent of tramway expansion, concerned about cost vs. schools and fire brigades
- **Chief Transit Engineer Yara Okonkwo** — Designed the tramway expansion, studied systems in Verlaine, Port Caravel, Ashford Republic

## Law Enforcement & Emergency Services
- **Fire Marshal Edwin Hale** — Investigating Greystone Wharf fire, identified accelerant
- **Battalion Chief Cora Whitmore** — Bobington Fire Brigade
- **Inspector Helena Greaves** — Metropolitan Constabulary, coordinating security for Rovers cup parade

## Diplomats & Foreign Figures
- **Consul Elara Miren** — Thessarine ambassador to Bobington (Ashbury Lane consulate)
- **Ambassador Evren Soldt** — Thessarine ambassador to Delvaria, recalled amid Kaelmar crisis
- **Foreign Secretary Alaric Daine** — Thessarine Confederation, announced ambassador recall

## Business & Commerce
- **Gerald Ashcroft** — Managing director of Ashcroft Property Group. Under suspicion in Greystone Wharf fire. Denies involvement.
- **Clement Varga** — Senior commodities analyst at Fernwich Trading House
- **Felix Rensler** — Proprietor of Rensler's coffee house on Threadneedle Street

## Sports Figures
- **Kael Dunmore** — Bobington Rovers midfielder. Scored winning Merchants' Cup goal from 35 yards, 118th minute. Under contract, transfer talk dismissed.
- **Phillipa Corbett** — Rovers manager. Turbulent season with calls for dismissal before cup win.
- **Sully Marsh** — Rovers goalkeeper
- **Orin Blackshaw** — Rovers centre-back, scored the equaliser in cup final
- **Nadia Osei** — Rovers player, assisted Dunmore's winner
- **Magnus Stahl** — Ironhall United goalkeeper
- **Brennan Cole** — Ironhall United star striker, scored their cup final goal

## Arts & Culture
- **Isolde Hargrove** — Painter, born 1951 in Dunbar's Reach. Reclusive, lives in Millhaven. Studied at Bobington Academy under Matthias Crane. Known for luminous landscapes and intimate portraits. Retrospective "Light Through Glass" at Royal Bobington Gallery (120+ works, through 30 June).
- **Matthias Crane** — Legendary painter, Hargrove's teacher, known for brooding seascapes
- **Simone Aldair** — Director of the Royal Bobington Gallery

## Historical Preservation
- **Louisa Marchbank** — Chairwoman of the Bobington Historical Preservation Society

## Private Citizens
- **Captain Runa Halverssen** — Cargo brig *Windhaven*, reported Delvarian naval buildup
- **Estelle Danforth** — Commuter from Docklands to Upper Fernwich, bookkeeper
- **Doyle** — Plumber known to Pemberton, affected by copper price rises
- **Marta Selwyn** — Tram conductor from the Docklands, visited Hargrove exhibition with teenage daughter
- **Tobias Renn** — Elderly farmer in Millhaven, subject of Hargrove portrait (2018)
