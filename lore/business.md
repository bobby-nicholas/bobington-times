# Business & Commerce

This file tracks the commercial world of Bobington — major firms, trade, markets, and economic developments beyond the macro-level coverage in `economy.md`.

## Major Firms & Enterprises

- **Ashcroft Property Group** — Under investigation. See `people.md`, `institutions.md`.
- **Merchants' Guild of Bobington** — Powerful trade body representing major commercial interests. Current Guildmaster: Phineas Craddock.
- **Ironhall Steelworks** — Major industrial concern, based in Ironhall (see `geography.md`). Key employer.
- **The Bobington Exchange** — The city's commodities and securities exchange, located on Threadneedle Lane. Trades copper futures, grain, textiles, government bonds.
- **Greystone Shipping & Haulage** — Docklands-based logistics firm. One of several firms affected by the wharf fire investigation.

## Trade Routes & Partners

- Key exports: manufactured goods, textiles, processed metals
- Key imports: raw copper (from Greymoor Ranges and abroad), grain, timber, luxury goods
- Major trading partners: Thessara, the Ashford Republic, Kaelmar port cities, Fenmouth

## Markets & Indicators

- **Copper futures**: Traded on the Bobington Exchange. Currently at 886 florins/tonne (volatile due to Kaelmar crisis).
- **The Exchange Index**: A composite of the top 40 firms traded on the Exchange.
- **Grain prices**: Seasonal, affected by weather and shipping disruption.

## Business Personalities

- **Clive Haldane** — Managing director of Haldane & Webb, age 56. Founder's grandson. Cautious but committed; accepted the 500-machine Ashford Republic order despite scaling concerns.
- **Dorothy Haldane** — Manages accounts at Haldane & Webb. Pragmatic optimist. "We've survived two wars, a factory fire in 1943..."
- **Bernard Tull** — Oldest employee at Haldane & Webb, age 67, 42 years of service. Key-lever specialist. Philosophical about the future of the trade.
