# Economy & Commerce

## Currency
- **The florin** — Bobington's currency. Subdivided into centimes (50-centime tram fare mentioned).

## Key Economic Facts
- **Copper** — Major import via Kaelmar Strait (~1/3 of supply). Futures at 847 florins/tonne, up 14% (12-year high) due to Kaelmar crisis.
- **Eastern spice trade** — ~half passes through Kaelmar Strait
- **Coffee at Rensler's** — 4 florins (up from 3.60) due to shipping cost increases

## Major Projects
- **Veridan Corridor Tramway Expansion** — 14 miles, Docklands to Upper Fernwich via Midtown. 12 stations. Cost: 3.2 billion florins. Phase 1 breaks ground autumn 2026. Full completion spring 2030. 6,000 workers at peak. Greymoor granite for facades. Chief Engineer: Yara Okonkwo. Copper price rises threaten the budget.
